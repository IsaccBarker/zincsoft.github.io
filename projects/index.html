Catnet (huge effort in progress!)

CATnet is a more desentralized web for a more modern age. It acts like TOR in that it distributes your network packets, but instead of with trusted servers, it distributes your traffic through machines locally connected to the network in an ecrypted fashion. Because CATnet distributes network traffic, it has the side effect of speeding up the interest. CATnets architechure allows the owner of a catnet server to block certain users and websites, without violating their identity. For this reason, we plan to deploy to our school once development has reached v1.0.0
